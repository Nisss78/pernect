# Pernect ブランドイメージ生成プロンプト集

## ブランドコンセプト

**Pernect = Person + Connect**

「AIと繋がることで、自分の深層と繋がる」

診断を重ねるたびに自分のコンテキストが蓄積され、AIが本当の自分を映し出す。
未来的でクールだけど、人間味を失わない。若者が「いけてる」と感じるプロダクト。

```
Minimalist logo design for "Pernect" - a self-discovery AI app.
```

## アプリUI画面プロンプト

### Home Screen（ホーム画面）
```
Mobile app UI design for "Pernect" - a premium AI self-discovery app.

Home screen showing:
- Clean top header with subtle logo and notification icon
- Large hero card with current "self-insight" or daily reflection
- Horizontally scrolling diagnosis cards with consistent visual language
- Each card has a subtle icon, title, and progress indicator
- Bottom navigation with floating action button

Color palette:
- Background: Off-white (#F8FAFC)
- Primary accent: Deep indigo (#4F46E5)
- Secondary: Soft purple (#8B5CF6)
- Cards: White with subtle shadow
- Text: Charcoal (#1E293B)

Style: Premium, clean, spacious, modern tech aesthetic

Think: Notion meets Apple Health meets premium fintech app
```

```
Color palette:
- Background: Dark mode - deep navy (#0F172A) with subtle gradient
- Accent: Glowing purple (#A78BFA) and blue (#60A5FA)

Style: Futuristic, premium, feels like receiving important insights
```

## 追加のスタイル指示

必要に応じて以下を追加:

```
--ar 9:16 (モバイル縦長)
--ar 1:1 (ロゴ・アイコン)
--style raw (より写実的)
--v 6.1 (Midjourney最新版)

Negative prompt suggestions:
- no cartoon style
- no cute illustrations
- no clinical/medical feel
- no generic stock photo feel
- no overly bright colors
- no cluttered design
```
